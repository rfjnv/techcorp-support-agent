TechCorp Returns Policy

Eligible returns: Most products may be returned within 30 days of delivery if unused and in original packaging.

Non-returnable items: Digital downloads, gift cards, and opened software licenses.

Refund timing: Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.

Return shipping: TechCorp provides a prepaid label for defective items. For change-of-mind returns, customers pay return shipping.

How to start a return: Log in at support.techcorp.com/returns or email support@techcorp.com with your order number.
