TechCorp Shipping Information

Free shipping: Orders over $50 qualify for free standard shipping within the continental United States.

Standard delivery: 5–7 business days via ground carriers.

Express delivery: 2–3 business days. Available at checkout for an additional fee.

Order tracking: Tracking numbers are emailed within 24 hours of shipment. Track at support.techcorp.com/track.

International shipping: Available to Canada and the EU. Duties and taxes may apply and are the customer's responsibility.

Delivery issues: If a package is lost or damaged in transit, contact support@techcorp.com within 14 days of the estimated delivery date.
