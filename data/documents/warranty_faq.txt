TechCorp Warranty FAQ

Standard warranty: All hardware includes a 1-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects.

Extended warranty: TechCare+ extends coverage to 3 years and includes accidental damage protection. Purchase within 60 days of the original order.

What is covered: Defective parts, battery failure below 80% capacity in year one, and power adapter failures.

What is not covered: Cosmetic damage, liquid damage (unless TechCare+), unauthorized repairs, and normal wear.

Claims process: Open a ticket at support@techcorp.com with proof of purchase and a description of the issue. Response time is 1–2 business days.
